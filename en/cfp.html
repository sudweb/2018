Share an experience!

This year, the call for proposals concerns all interventions at Sud-Web 2018.

There will still be a form of curation, but all subjects will go through this form so that the team can choose the programming in a concerted way.

All forms of talks are possible:

  • talk with visual support(s);
  • lighting talk ;
  • stand-up ;
  • group activities…

We do not want you to feel trapped in the elaboration of a "traditional" talk. Let's find formats that make sense with which you feel comfortable!

Do you have one or more ideas? Do not hesitate, you have until: Feburary 16, 2018 (11:59 pm, Paris Time) to propose a talk.

We remind you that we'll favour:

  • experience feedbacks ;
  • what make sense in your approach ;
  • what you have learned from this experience, the lesson(s) you learned from it ;
  • the change to be applied from the following Monday.

Afraid to jump in? Feel free to contact us, we will accompany you so that you are ready on the D day D!
